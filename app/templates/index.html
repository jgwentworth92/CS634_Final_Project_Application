Medical Health System (MHS) Dashboard

Employee and Facility Management

Provides tools for managing employees, medical offices, outpatient surgery facilities, and interactions with insurance companies. This application facilitates the insertion, updating, and viewing of information related to various entities involved in the healthcare provision process.

Patient Management

Enables comprehensive management of patient records, including the creation of new records, scheduling and updating of appointments, and handling of medical billing and procedures. This section ensures efficient patient flow and billing accuracy.

Management Reporting

Provides essential reporting tools that support management in making informed decisions. This includes generating reports on daily revenues by facility, listing appointments by physician and date, and analyzing the financial performance over selected periods. These capabilities are crucial for strategic planning and financial management.
